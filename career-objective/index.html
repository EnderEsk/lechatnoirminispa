Inspired by Maya Angelou's words, I believe the most meaningful work is in making people feel valued, beautiful, and cared for. Completing my Esthetics course expanded my skills and gave me a creative, hands-on way to care for clients. I discovered both where my strengths lie and how much joy I find in services such as manicures, pedicures, waxing, lash lifts, eyelash extensions, and professional makeup artistry.

I am currently preparing a home-based esthetics studio where I will be offering a range of services starting October. In the meantime, I am seeking opportunities with organizations such as Shoppers or London Drugs to gain experience, continue growing, and share my knowledge of skin care and makeup with clients.

I view esthetics as a lifelong learning journey. My goal is to keep expanding my knowledge and skills so I can provide the highest level of care and artistry. I am also open to refining my path further should the right opportunity present itself.
